Flappy Birds
Project

Developed a web-based rendition of the popular mobile game Flappy Birds; the application offers an engaging experience, mirroring the original gameplay mechanics.

  • Keyboard controls: Use the Up and Down arrow keys to control the bird.
  • Touch controls: Touch the top half of the screen to make the bird fly up or the bottom half of the screen to make the bird go down.
  • Mouse controls: Left click to make the bird rise and Right click for the bird to go down.

Connect Four
Project

Connect Four app using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
Use the Arrow keys for navigation:

  • ArrowLeft: Move disc to the left
  • ArrowRight: Move disc to the right
  • ArrowDown or Enter: Drop the disc into the grid

On a touch device, touch the left or right half of the screen to move the disc left or right respectively. Touch the bottom half of the screen to drop the disc into the grid.
